About

Design with Kad.

An initiative to help young individuals start and uplift their graphic design career at the Federal University of Technology, Akure. We are starting with a small number of inquisitive individuals. Let's see what the future holds.

Meet the Host

Katola Ayomipo
Dickson

Graphic Designer · Creator · Storyteller

Katola Ayomipo Dickson is a graphic designer, growth enthusiast, creator, storyteller and a 200 Level student of Information Technology in FUTA. He's passionate about building a design culture in FUTA giving more people the opportunity to express themselves visually. Through Design with Kad, he brings structured, hands-on design education to students at FUTA.

With a focus on visual communication, brand identity, and practical tool mastery, Kad believes the best designers are first taught how to think, then how to create.

FAQ

Common Questions.

Everything you need to know about
Design with Kad before you join.

Is the course really free?
+

Design with Kad is completely free.

What should I bring to class?
+

Your laptop or smartphone, a notebook, and an open mind ready to learn.

Do I need prior design experience?
+

Yes and No. If you're a beginner, we start from the fundamentals and progressively build your skill. If you already have prior design experience, the Design with Kad session would still work for you.

What software will we use?
+

We will be using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma and a couple of others. Smartphone designers are welcome to use their phones.

When are the classes held?
+

3pm on Sundays at the School of Computing, FUTA, Akure. Specific dates and times would be shared with proper notification.
